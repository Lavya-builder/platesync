## 3. Product Hunt Launch

Make an account at https://www.producthunt.com, then create a post at https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new

**Tagline:** Offline-first PWA calorie tracker with 160+ Indian foods — no account needed

**Description:**
PlateSync is a privacy-focused, offline-first Progressive Web App for tracking calories and macros. Unlike MyFitnessPal or Cronometer, PlateSync requires no account, no internet, and no data collection.

Key features:
- 160+ verified Indian foods (dals, curries, breads, snacks, fruits, veggies)
- Barcode scanner with Indian product data fallback
- Per-day date navigation with history
- Edit/delete entries
- Water tracker
- Favorites & saved meals
- Weekly/monthly analytics
- Adaptive calorie goals
- Meal prep planner
- CSV export & JSON backup
- Dark mode
- Installable PWA — works fully offline

Perfect for anyone tracking Indian cuisine who's tired of inaccurate calorie data in mainstream apps.

**First comment (post after publishing):**
Built this because I couldn't find a calorie tracker with decent Indian food data. Most apps show mango as 302 kcal instead of ~60, or miss basic items like dal, roti, and dosa. PlateSync has 160+ verified Indian foods and works completely offline — no account, no ads, no cloud. Would love feedback!
